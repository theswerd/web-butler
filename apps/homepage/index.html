Questions

Does this follow the ChatGPT/Grok/Claude Terms of Service?

Maybe, probably not. I never checked.

How much does it cost?

Nothing. You bring the AI subscription you already pay for; the butler just puts it to work.

Where do my prompts go?

To your own AI account, through a private sandbox tied to you. Web Butler has no model of its own and no reason to keep your data.

Can it break a website?

Only for you, and only until you flip the extension off. Alterations are per-user scripts in your browser, not edits to the actual site.

Is it open source?

Fully. The whole service is at theswerd/web-butler.
